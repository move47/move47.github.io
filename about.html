About me

Hi, I’m Himanshu Goyal. I am a Dual Degree student at Indian Institute of Technology Bhubaneswar in the School of Electrical Sciences, majoring in Computer Science and Engineering. I work closely with Prof. Sudipta Saha on practical and scalability aspects of having Byzantine consensus in low power wireless systems. Along with this, I am also working on security & privacy aspects for large scale decentralised systems. Here, I am focussing on developing privacy-preserving solutions for efficient distributive computation. I am also closely associated with Decentralized and Smart Systems Research Group (DSSRG) at IIT Bhubaneswar.

I’ve spent some excellent summers in both industry and academia via internships. In Summer 2020, I interned with Embedded Systems and Robotics group team at TCS Research & Innovation with Chayan Sarkar on building customized language model for cognitive robotics application. I also had a short stint working with Arpita Patra at Cryptography and Information Security lab, IISc Bangalore in 2020. In Summer 2019 I worked on workload characterization in Multi-tier Cloud Infrastructure at CNeRG - Complex Networks Research Lab, IIT Kharagpur under the mentorship of Sandip Chakraborty.

When I’m not working, I am generally lurking reddit and twitter for keeping myself updated about the recent trends in my research work. I would highly recommend to follow known researchers of your field for getting fast access to latest research directions. I like to collect resources for the benefit of fledgling students under Crypto Resources and Miscellaneous section. The resources may range from basic to advanced level. I also maintain a blog which sporadically gets updated.

During my undergraduate days, I was associated with E-Summit, where I both participated and organised several events related to Entrepreneurship. I was also member of The Fourth Wall(Dramatics Society of IIT Bhubaneswar), where I took part in several open and stage acts. I completely enjoyed being here, I am and will cherish it throughout my life.
